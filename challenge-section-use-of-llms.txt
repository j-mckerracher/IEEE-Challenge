Use of LLMs

Participants may use Large Language Models (LLMs) or other AI tools if allowed by the official competition rules. Any use of these tools should be disclosed in the methodology report.

If LLMs or AI tools are used, participants should briefly explain:

- which tools were used
- what parts of the workflow they supported
- whether they were used for coding, debugging, feature ideas, documentation, model design, or analysis
- how generated code or suggestions were checked
- whether the tools had any meaningful impact on the final solution

Participants remain responsible for the correctness, originality, and rule compliance of their submissions. AI-generated code should be reviewed, tested, and understood by the team before submission.

Transparency is important. Disclosure of AI tool usage is not intended to penalize participants automatically; it helps judges evaluate the originality, methodology, and reliability of the submitted work.
